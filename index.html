Our Eye care Services

See the World in a Whole New Way with Our Eye Care Services.

Comprehensive eye exams

Regular comprehensive eye exams are essential to maintaining optimal eye health and clear vision.

Eyeglass and sunglass fitting and prescription

With a wide selection of frames and lenses from top brands, we're sure to have the perfect fit for you.

Low vision care

Our experienced low vision specialists will work with you to develop a customized treatment plan that helps you maximize your remaining vision.

Diabetic eye exam

If you have diabetes, it's essential to have regular diabetic eye exams to protect your vision and detect any potential problems early.

Specialty lenses

If you have a specific vision need or eye condition, an eye care center may offer specialty lenses such as toric lenses for astigmatism, multifocal lenses for presbyopia, or scleral lenses for irregular corneas.

Cleaning and maintenance

Proper cleaning and maintenance of your glasses is essential for maintaining optimal vision and prolonging the life of your glasses.
